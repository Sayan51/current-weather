Welcome to the Weather App

This Weather App provides current weather information for any city you search for. It uses the WeatherAPI service to fetch real-time weather data including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.

The app also includes additional pages for weather forecasts, user settings, and information about the app itself.

Created as a simple project to demonstrate HTML, CSS, and JavaScript integration for a weather application.

Feel free to explore the app and provide feedback!
